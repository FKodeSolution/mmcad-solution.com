Our Approval Process

Streamlined workflow for fast and reliable building approvals

Initial Consultation

We assess your requirements and explain the approval process, documentation needed, and timeline for your specific project.

Site Survey & Measurements

Our licensed surveyors conduct a thorough site analysis to collect all necessary measurements and verify property boundaries.

CAD Design Development

We create detailed architectural drawings, structural designs, and all required plans for submission to the corporation.

Documentation & Application

Our team prepares and submits all necessary documents, including forms, affidavits, and certificates with the application.

Approval Processing

We monitor the application process and address any queries or requests from the authorities promptly to avoid delays.

Approval & Commencement

Once approved, we provide you with the complete set of sanctioned drawings and documents to begin your construction.
